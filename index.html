The Co-Hosting Business Model Explained

A simple 3-step system that anyone can implement

1

Partner with Property Owners (No Buying or Leasing)

Find property owners who want higher income but don't want the hassle of managing short-term rentals. You'll offer to boost their income by 30-100% compared to traditional long-term rentals while taking all the work off their hands. We show you exactly how to find these owners and convince them to say yes.

2

Manage Everything Remotely

Handle all aspects of the rental remotely including listing creation, pricing optimization, guest communication, and coordinating cleaning and maintenance. Our systems automate 80% of the work, allowing you to manage multiple properties efficiently from anywhere with internet access.

3

Collect 20-30% Commission

For every booking, you keep 20-30% of the rental income. With just 10 properties under management, you can easily generate $10,000+ per month in commission income. And because you've built systems that scale, adding new properties increases your income without proportionally increasing your workload.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about starting a co-hosting business

Do I need experience in Airbnb or property management? +

No, our comprehensive blueprint provides everything you need to start from scratch. The documents and training materials are designed to guide complete beginners through the entire process, from finding your first client to scaling your business. All the templates, scripts, and systems are included so you don't need to have prior experience.

How quickly can I expect to find my first property to manage? +

Many of our clients secure their first property within 30 days of implementing our marketing materials and client acquisition strategies. The blueprint includes multiple proven methods for finding property owners, including direct outreach, networking with real estate agents, identifying underperforming listings, and leveraging existing vacation homeowners. The professional documents instantly give you credibility, making it easier to convert prospects into clients.

How much time do I need to dedicate to this business? +

Initially, you'll spend most of your time acquiring properties and setting up systems (approximately 10-15 hours per week). Once established, each property typically requires just 1-2 hours of management per week thanks to our automation systems. A portfolio of 10 properties can be managed in 10-20 hours per week while generating $7,000-$15,000 in monthly income. The business is designed to be highly efficient with your time.

Are the documents customizable for my own business? +

Yes, all 22 documents come in fully editable formats. You can easily add your company name, logo, and adjust any details to fit your specific market or preferences. The documents are designed to be templates that you can personalize to match your brand and business needs. They've been battle-tested by successful co-hosts across diverse markets.

What if the property owner already uses Airbnb themselves? +

This is actually a great opportunity - owners who are already using Airbnb but struggling with the management are perfect prospects. Our blueprint includes specific strategies for approaching these owners and demonstrating how your professional management can improve their performance, increase their ratings, and free up their time. We include scripts specifically for convincing DIY hosts to switch to your management.
